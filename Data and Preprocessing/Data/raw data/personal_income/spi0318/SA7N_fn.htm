SA7N Footnotes

  • 2/ Under the 2007 NAICS, internet publishing and broadcasting was reclassified to other information services.

  • (D) Not shown to avoid disclosure of confidential information, but the estimates for this item are included in the total.

  • (T) Estimate for wages and salaries suppressed to cover corresponding estimate for earnings. Estimates for this item are included in the total.
  • (NA) Data not available for this year.
  • (NM) Not meaningful.
